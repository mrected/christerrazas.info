About

Hi, I’m Chris Terrazas. I’ve been a Graphic Artist/Photographer/Art Director for a long time. About 2 years ago I realized I was bored with it. Nothing’s hard anymore. Nothing needs figuring out. Every problem is easily solvable. In short, it’s very boring.

I decided to move into programming. So far it’s been constantly challenging and I enjoy programming much more than I thought I would (I’m not a “math person” has been a goto excuse for a while now). I’ve been focused more in Web Development. I’m pretty well versed in HTML5, JavaScript, jQuery, CSS and I’m currently making some headway into WordPress and Python.

I’ve got a couple sites up so far, including this one, and I’m looking to move into a career that focuses on development instead of graphics. I just like programming a lot more. So, check out what I’ve done. If any of it looks appealing to you, get a hold of me, I’m up for the challenge and I would love to be a part of your company/team/whatever has a focus in these areas.
